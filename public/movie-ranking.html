The Best Movies According to Hugo

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Face Off

One of my favorite actors is Nicholas Cage and I think this is one of his best movies hands down. It's got action and a good story plot. I love it!

I Love You, Man

This is my favorite comedy feel good movie. It's really funny and it's so enjoyable that I can put it on and watch it over and over again.

Avengers: End Game

This movie was the colmination of several differnt movies combined into one final chapter. I thought it was really creative and nicely done. I haven't seen anything like that before. It gave me goosebumps at the end.
